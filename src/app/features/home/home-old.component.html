About Movie & TV Bookmark

A simple skill showcase web-app for searching and saving movies and tv shows in dedicated bookmarks. Explore and update your movies and tv shows saved in the bookmarks section as you wish! You can also remove a media from the bookmark.

  • Watchlist Bookmark

    Save movies and tv shows that you'd like to watch someday!

  • Watched Bookmark

    A list of movies and tv show that you've already watched, just to keep in mind!

  • Rewatch Bookmark

    A list of movies and tv show that you'd like to watch again!

  • Still Watching Bookmark

    A list of movies and tv show that you haven't finished yet!
